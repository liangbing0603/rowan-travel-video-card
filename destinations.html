Travel Planning Tips

Visa & Documentation

Check visa requirements at least 3 months before your trip. Keep digital copies of important documents.

Budget Planning

Research average costs for accommodation, food, and activities. Always have a contingency fund.

Travel Insurance

Never travel without insurance. It covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost luggage.

Sustainable Travel

Choose eco-friendly accommodations, support local businesses, and minimize plastic use.
