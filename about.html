About Rowan

Traveler, storyteller, and advocate for sustainable adventures

Hello, I'm Rowan!

Welcome to Wanderlust Adventures, my personal travel blog where I share stories, tips, and insights from my journeys around the world. What started as a simple travel diary has grown into a community of fellow adventurers seeking authentic, sustainable, and meaningful travel experiences.

Based in Shanghai but constantly on the move, I've visited over 40 countries across 6 continents, always with a focus on connecting with local cultures, minimizing environmental impact, and finding those hidden gems that don't make it into guidebooks.

My Travel Journey

2018

The Beginning

My first solo trip to Southeast Asia. Three months backpacking through Thailand, Vietnam, and Cambodia taught me the basics of budget travel and ignited my passion for exploration.

2020

Digital Nomad Life

Started working remotely as a content creator, allowing me to travel while working. Spent 6 months in Europe, balancing work with weekend adventures.

2022

Sustainable Focus

After witnessing environmental challenges in popular destinations, I shifted my focus to sustainable travel practices and began advocating for responsible tourism.

2024

Community Building

Started this blog to share knowledge and build a community of like-minded travelers. Began hosting virtual meetups and creating detailed destination guides.

2026

Present Day

Continuing to explore while focusing on creating valuable content for young travelers. Currently planning expeditions to lesser-known destinations in Central Asia.

My Travel Philosophy

Sustainable Travel

I believe in traveling in a way that respects local communities and minimizes environmental impact. This means choosing eco-friendly accommodations, supporting local businesses, and being mindful of my carbon footprint.

Cultural Exchange

Travel should be about genuine connection. I prioritize meaningful interactions with locals, learning about their traditions, and sharing stories rather than just ticking off tourist attractions.

Slow Travel

Quality over quantity. I prefer spending more time in fewer places to truly understand and appreciate them, rather than rushing through a checklist of destinations.

Knowledge Sharing

What I learn on the road should benefit others. I'm committed to creating honest, practical content that helps fellow travelers have better, more responsible adventures.
